Approach

Our approach to original IP entails:
A. a sharp idea about modern life;
B. storytelling with engaging characters;
C. rich world-building;
D. stories told through nonlinear media with focus on gaming.
We call this approach Constellation Storytelling.

History

2012/2013

Four highschool friends made small experimental mobile games.

2017

A bigger team of seven decided to make
Golf Club: Wasteland as the first commercial endevour.
In December, Demagog Studio was founded as a company.

2018

In the summer, Golf Club: Wasteland was published as an Apple,
drawing attention for its world-building and original soundtrack
Radio Nostalgia from Mars.

2021

The Cub and Highwater were announced.
The studio has grown to twenty people.
The Cub premiered at the Tribeca Film Festival in New York.

2023

Highwater was released on Netflix in March to positive reviews.
Pocketgamer nomination for mobile game of the year.
Golf Club: Wasteland renamed on all storefronts to Golf Club: Nostalgia.
The Cub date trailer published.

2024

The Cub published on Sony PlayStation, Nintendo Switch and PC with a 9/10 score on Steam.
The original soundtrack Radio Nostalgia from Mars: The Cub released on all music platforms.
Highwater released on PC and consoles to positive reviews. The OST Highwater Pirate Radio available across music platforms.
